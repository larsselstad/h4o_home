H4O

The idea

Ghost traps, ghost fishing and littering of the ocean are a huge international problem. H4O is a social community app that connects divers, dive clubs, environmental organizations, fishermen and -women, with a goal to inspire and coordinate. The app uses open map data to help visualize the effort.
